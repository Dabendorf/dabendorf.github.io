Til Topps Bergen, Frivillighetsarbeid

One of the most amazing projects existing in Norway is the hiking group of Til Topps. It is a group of volunteers going on hikes around Bergen and Hordaland, taking with us people from other countries wanting to learn Norwegian. I once myself learned Norwegian that way and it is a great pleasure, helping people getting better in speaking Norwegian in a stress-free environment (at least if you think hiking is stress-free).
If you are interested in becoming a volunteer as well or want to know more about the group, you can visit our website or send me an e-mail.
